I am a graduate of BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus, where I pursued a B.E. in Computer Science and Msc. in Economics.

I have been fortunate enough to be advised by some of the best researchers in the field of AI, including Amit Sheth and T.K. Prasad at Kno.e.sis, Karim Jerbi from Cognitive & Computational Neuroscience Lab, University of Montreal, and Samira Kahou from MILA. Previously, I also worked as a consultant at MILA advised by Yoshua Bengio. Recently, I used to work as a Research Associate at Google in the Machine Learning and Optimization Dept. with Dheeraj Nagaraj, and have currently transitioned to a Pre-Doctoral Researcher role at the same organization under the supervision of Prateek Jain and Inderjit Dhillon.

My research interests lie broadly in generalization, causality, theoretical machine learning, and real-world representation learning.

I also use this website to blog about research that interests me. In my spare time, I enjoy gaming and playing badminton.
